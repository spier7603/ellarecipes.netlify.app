##### The ingredients needed to make Raspberry Thumbprint Cookies With Lemon Icing:

1. Get 2/3 cup granulated sugar
1. Make ready 2 cup flour
1. Make ready 1 splash of vanilla
1. Get 1/4 cup raspberry jam
1. Take 1 cup powdered sugar
1. Make ready 1 lemon
1. Prepare 2 tbsp of water or more
1. Prepare 1 splash of vanilla
1. Prepare 2 stick butter softened




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Raspberry Thumbprint Cookies With Lemon Icing:

1. In your mixer creme sugar, vanilla,butter together.on low speed add flour and combine, increase speed untill done forms.
1. Remove dough and flatten on a piece of plastic wrap. Referigerate for 1 hour.
1. Meanwhile preheat oven to 350 . Start making icing.
1. In small bowl combine sugar , juice of 1/2 of the lemon, water, vanilla. Whisk till combined. ( you want it to be able to drizzle. Might need to add a little extra water.) Pour icing in a small plastic bag.put in the refigerator to keep chilled.
1. Remove dough .Shape into1 inch balls. Make a small print into the cookies. Stir jam to loosen it up.put a little in each cookie.
1. Bake 15-17 minutes.until lightly brown on the edges. Cool completely.
1. Once cookies have cooled.cut a tiny opening in the tip of your bag to create tip. Drizzle with icing on top. Let icing get hard.
