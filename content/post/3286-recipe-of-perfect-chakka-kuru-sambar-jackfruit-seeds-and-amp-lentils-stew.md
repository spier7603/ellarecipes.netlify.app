##### The ingredients needed to make Chakka kuru Sambar / Jackfruit Seeds &amp; Lentils Stew:

1. Prepare 1 cup Chakka kuru / Jackfruit seeds
1. Get 1/2 cup Toor dal
1. Take 1 small Tamarind  ,lemon sized ball
1. Make ready 1 tsp Turmeric powder
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Sambar powder
1. Get 2 Dry red chilies
1. Get 1 tsp Mustard seeds
1. Get 1 sprig Curry leaves
1. Make ready As needed Water
1. Take As needed Salt
1. Make ready As needed Oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chakka kuru Sambar / Jackfruit Seeds &amp; Lentils Stew:

1. Soak the tamarind in water for 10-15 mins, extract the juice and keep it aside. Clean the chakka kuru by scrapping the outer covering of the seeds. Wash the chakka kuru and toor dal separately.
1. In a pressure cooker, cook the jackfruit seeds and toor dal for 4-5 whistles or until done. Remove from flame and keep it aside. Once the pressure has subsided, remove the lid.
1. Heat oil in a pan. Splutter mustard seeds, dry red chilies, and curry leaves. Add the turmeric powder and sambar powder and fry for 1 min. Add the cooked jackfruit seeds &amp; toor dal mixture and mix well. Add 1 cup of water and bring it to a boil. Add the tamarind extract and mix well. Allow it to simmer for a few minutes. Remove from flame and garnish with more fresh curry leaves. Serve hot chakka kuru sambar with steamed rice or dosa.
